Frequently asked questions

        

        

          

            
Is OpenF1 official?

            

              No. OpenF1 is an independent, community-driven project not
              associated with Formula 1, FIA, or Formula One Management.
            

          

          

            
What's the data delay?

            

              Real-time data typically updates about 3 seconds after live events
              ‚Äî faster than most TV broadcasts.
            

          

          

            

              How far back does historical data go?
            

            

              Data is available from the 2023 season onwards.
            

          

          

            
Are there rate limits?

            

              Yes, but they're generous. The free tier allows 3 req/s and 30
              req/min, plenty for most projects. Paid plans double that.
            

          

          

            
What formats are supported?

            

              JSON (default) and CSV. Add "csv=true" to any request for
              spreadsheet-compatible output.
            

          

          

            

              What counts as "live" vs "historical" data?
            

            

              Live data is anything from a session still in progress or ended
              less than 30 minutes ago. After that, it's historical and free to
              access.
